Hi, I'm Malin — the person behind Kids Gonna Play

Hej, jag är Malin – personen bakom Kids Gonna Play

I live in Sweden and I'm often described as playful and curious — I've never wanted to let go of the imaginative, wonder-filled way kids naturally see the world. With Kids Gonna Play, I want to help both kids and grown-ups rediscover the power of play and shared time together, and make a few more memories along the way. I dug through my own childhood memories and researched games and traditions from around the world to put this book together.

Jag bor i Sverige och beskrivs ofta som lekfull och nyfiken – jag har aldrig velat släppa det kreativa och fantasifulla sätt som barn naturligt ser på världen.
